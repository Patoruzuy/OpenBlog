About contribution identities

When you submit a revision or comment, OpenBlog snapshots your current identity mode. This means:

  • Public — your display name and avatar are shown.
  • Pseudonymous — your alias is shown instead of your real name.
  • Anonymous — shown as "Anonymous" with no identifying information.

Changing your identity mode affects future contributions only. You can update your pseudonymous alias on the Privacy settings page.
