Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Data we collect

  • Account data — email address, username, and hashed password when you register.
  • Content data — posts, comments, and revisions you create.
  • Usage data — server logs including IP addresses and page views for security and analytics.

How we use your data

  • To provide and operate the OpenBlog service.
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, notifications) you've requested.
  • To detect and prevent abuse.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data when required by law.

Data retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication. See our Cookie Policy.

Contact

For privacy-related requests, email privacy@openblog.dev.
