Editorial Policy

Last updated: January 2025

OpenBlog is committed to publishing accurate, well-reasoned technical content. This policy describes our standards for published posts, revisions, and comments.

Publication standards

  • All claims should be verifiable and backed by evidence or working code.
  • Posts must be original work. Plagiarism will result in immediate removal.
  • Marketing content, advertisements, and paid placements are not permitted.

Revision policy

Any registered user with a verified email may propose a revision to any published post. Revisions should:

  • Correct factual errors or out-of-date information.
  • Improve clarity without changing the author's voice or intent.
  • Not change the fundamental argument or conclusion of the post without author agreement.

Authors are solely responsible for accepting or declining revisions to their posts. The editorial team may step in for posts whose authors are unreachable.

Comments

Comments must be constructive and on-topic. Personal attacks, spam, and off-topic promotion are removed without notice.

Corrections

If you spot an error in a published post, please submit a revision or contact us directly.
