About OpenBlog

OpenBlog is an open platform for engineers who believe every piece of technical knowledge is worth preserving — and worth improving.

Our mission

Software is collaborative by nature. A tutorial that helped you today may have a subtle error that trips up the next reader. OpenBlog gives every reader the ability to propose revisions — and every author the right to accept them — so the knowledge base gets better over time rather than stale.

How it works

  • Read — all published posts are publicly accessible, no account required.
  • Contribute — create a free account to comment, vote, and propose revisions.
  • Write — apply for contributor access to publish your own posts.
  • Improve — spot a mistake? Submit a revision. Authors review and accept or decline.

Technology

OpenBlog is built with Flask, PostgreSQL, and Redis. The source code is available on GitHub under the MIT licence. We eat our own cooking.
